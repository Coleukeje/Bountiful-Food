About US

Bountiful Foods is a small company that specializes in organic and locally farmed foods. We are technically headquartered in Carlsbad, California, however our food trucks span the entire coast from Los Angeles to San Diego. Bountiful Foods distributes fruits and vegetables directly from local farmers, but it also works with national and international suppliers. Our owner-operated firm caters to walk-in traffic at our Carlsbad storefront and, of course, our food trucks. We deliver and manage those orders over the phone and through the website. Our objective is to give fresh and healthy food options, such as organic, made-to-order, fruit drinks, and fruit bags, to the local community and tourists visiting the coastal areas. Bountiful Foods was started by 4 farmers who got together with the same objective of providing healthy, fresh, and nutritious food and drinks to California residents.
